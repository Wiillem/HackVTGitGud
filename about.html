US Graduation Rates by State, 50%-90%


The average graduation rate in the US is 80%. Almost less than half of the states are under the average national graduation rate. However one should not compare one state’s graduation rate to another as every state has different standards for education. The states themselves are responsible for setting their own educational standard. If one wishes to compare graduation rates across states, average freshman graduation rate (AFGR) or NAEP scores should be used. Even then, comparisons should be made with caution as the state’s graduation rate does not reflect the academic abilities of the individual students.
